About BuildChangeOrder

BuildChangeOrder is a free online change order generator for freelancers, agencies, and contractors. Scope creep is one of the most common pain points in professional services — and the solution is documentation. BuildChangeOrder makes it effortless to generate a professional, signed change order PDF in seconds.

Every change order includes running contract totals (original contract + previous COs + this CO = new total), dual client and contractor approval signature blocks, optionally schedule impact days, and a reason-for-change classification. For developers, the BuildChangeOrder REST API provides programmatic generation on RapidAPI.

Part of the Build ecosystem alongside BuildQuotes, BuildInvoice, BuildPDF, and BuildCertificates.
